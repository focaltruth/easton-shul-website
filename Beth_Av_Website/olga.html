Olga Oretsky

Volunteer Administrative Assistant

Olga Oretsky is a genetic counselor specializing in oncology care and an active member of Beth Avraham. She holds a degree in biochemistry and psychology from the University of Pennsylvania and a Master of Science in Genetic Counseling, and previously conducted research at the National Institutes of Health. Olga brings her analytical skills and compassionate approach to both her professional work and her service to the synagogue community.

Olga and her husband, Jonathan, began learning with Rabbi Yitzchok Yagod before their wedding and have been regulars at Beth Avraham ever since, attending Shabbos and chagim. She provides supports for the administration and management of the shul, helping keep operations organized and welcoming for members and visitors alike.
