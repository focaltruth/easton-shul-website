WHO WE ARE

Easton’s Center for Torah

Beth Avraham is a Modern Orthodox synagogue in Easton, Pennsylvania, where tradition meets warmth and community. Our services blend Ashkenazi and Sephardi minhagim, reflecting the rich diversity of our members from around the world.

Whether you are Sephardi, Ashkenazi, Mizrachi, Bukharian, or Syrian, you’re welcome to lead and pray according to your customs. Every voice and tradition adds to the beauty of our shared tefillah.

At Beth Avraham, everyone belongs. We value connection, learning, and inclusion—reserving a board seat for younger members and welcoming new faces with genuine small-town friendliness. Come be part of our growing community.
